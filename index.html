about us

Seventh Shop Trading Est. has evolved from a small trading firm back in the early 1980’s to a major player in the electrical industry in terms of supply of electrical material and has also now established a firm base in consultancy services.

Founder and Director has an aim of not only seeing Seventh Shop Trading Est grow and prosper but also to provide our valuable customers the best of products available and complete consumer satisfaction. Today our goal is to not only expand on a major scale but also to generate more employment opportunities for the youth.

MACHINE SHOP

We have a full service machine shop allowing us to turn around repairs quickly.
